Transforming Graphs and Sketching
Understand and use graphs of functions; sketch curves defined by simple equations including polynomials
including horizontal and vertical asymptotes e.g. \(y = \dfrac{2}{x+a}+b\) has asymptotes \(y=b\) and \(x=-a\)
Understand and use inverse functions and their graphs
Understand the effect of simple transformations on the graph of \(y = f(x)\), including sketching associated graphs: \(y = af(x)\), \(y = f(x) + a\), \(y = f(x + a)\), \(y = f(ax)\) and combinations of these transformations e.g. \(y=-\cos\left(x+\dfrac{\pi}{4}\right)\)
